Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common home loan questions.

What documents do I need for a home loan?
You need ID proof, address proof, income proof, property papers, and bank statements. We help arrange missing documents.
What credit score do I need for a home loan?
We recommend a credit score of 700 or above for the best interest rates. A good credit score helps you qualify for lower rates and better loan terms.
How long does loan approval take?
7-10 days for approval, 15-20 days for disbursement. We expedite the process through our banking relationships.
Are there any hidden charges?
No hidden charges. We negotiate 100% processing fee waivers and provide full cost transparency.
What is balance transfer and how can it help me?
Balance transfer allows you to move your existing loan to a new lender offering lower interest rates. This can save you lakhs over your loan tenure.
Do you charge any fees for your services?
Our consultation is completely free. We earn commission from banks when your loan gets approved, so you pay nothing out of pocket.
